About Me

I started my coding journey in high school, back in 2010. My first language was C, and my first project was working with a team to program a small robot for the 2010 MIT Zero Robotics competition. Our team took fourth place nationally, and our code was even executed on the ISS for the final showdown!

After that, I went to college and took several coding related courses, in which I became familiar with the C++, Java, and Python languages. Object oriented programming was a bit of a paradigm shift for me coming from C, but I've come to enjoy the structure.

During my career, I've spent a good deal of time coding as a hobby on the side for various small projects, and I've found a good deal of applications for the AHK and VBA scripting languages. This was when I was introduced to Node.js, and I instantly fell in love with javascript (ES6 is a life saver).
